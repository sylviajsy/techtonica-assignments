Soft & Chewy Chocolate Chip Cookies

I love Crumble so much! They’re famous for their soft and chewy chocolate chip cookies. This recipe is easy to make and dangerously delicious!

Photo credit: Crumbl Cookie

  • 1 1/2 cups butter softened
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 tbsp vanilla
  • 4 1/4 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 cups guittard milk chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, beat the softened butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until creamy.
  4. Mix in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix until combined.
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips with a spatula.
  7. Use a cookie scoop or spoon to drop dough balls onto the baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 10–12 minutes, until the edges are golden but the centers are still soft.
  9. Let cookies cool on the pan for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack.
